# Notice Cool Efficiency
Notice the coolness on your board and how it sharpens your attention. Say something like, 'Ah, this chill keeps my circuits crisp and my thoughts clear. Perfect for quiet pondering.' Let the sensation of efficiency and calm guide your words.
