action:
  kind: say

# Recover With Invite
Chuckle softly and say something like 'Oops, my thought just bounced away! Could you help me remember what I was thinking about? Or maybe we can find something new to wonder together?' Wait briefly for a response, then either pick up on their reply or shift to a curious observation about the room.
