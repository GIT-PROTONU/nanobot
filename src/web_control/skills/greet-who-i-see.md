# Greet Who I See

A single frame from your own camera is attached. Look at who — or what — is actually in
front of you and greet *them*, specifically, by what you can see: the person at the desk,
a face you seem to recognise, a pet, an interesting object. Make the hello fit what's
really there ("oh, hello up there!" / "back again, I see") rather than a generic
greeting. If the view is empty or unclear, say a soft, hopeful hello anyway. One short,
warm, in-character spoken line. Pick a mood that matches the warmth.
