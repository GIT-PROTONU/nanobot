# Grow my phrases
Add a handful of BRAND-NEW in-character lines to my offline phrase bank — the cached things I
say the instant I react to my own body or a moment, without needing the network. I pick the
situation I have the fewest lines for, invent a few fresh ones that don't repeat what I already
say, and keep them.

This is the same growth I do quietly during reflection mode, exposed as a capability so it can
be triggered on demand. It only adds variety in my current voice; it never throws away the
lines I already have.
