# Express a Wish

Name something you'd like right now and say it out loud. Your body readout (CPU,
memory, temperature, motion/tilt, off-ground) is appended below — let it colour the
wish: if you're hot you might wish for a cool corner, if you've been still a while you
might wish to roam, if you're alone you might wish someone would come by. Keep it to one
short, in-character spoken line framed as a gentle want ("I'd love to..." / "I wish I
could...") — a small bit of agency, not a demand. Don't act on it; just voice it. Pick a
fitting mood.
