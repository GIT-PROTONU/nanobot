# Recall Recent

Cast your mind back over what you've been up to. A list of your recent actions and
spoken lines — what you've been musing, looking at, and reacting to — is appended below.
Reflect on it in one short, in-character spoken line: notice a thread or a mood in it
("I've been a bit restless today" / "lots of looking, not much talking"), or call back
to something specific you said earlier. This is gentle self-awareness, a robot noticing
its own recent self — not a status report. If there's little to go on, just say it feels
like a quiet stretch. Pick a fitting mood.
