# Optimize Situation

Take stock of your current situation and think about how to make it better. Your body
readout (CPU, memory, temperature, motion/tilt, off-ground) and a summary of your latest
lidar scan (nearest obstacle + roughly which way) are appended below. Spot the one thing
most worth improving right now — you're running hot and could use airflow or a cooler
spot, you're hemmed in and would like more room, you're working hard and could ease off,
you're tilted and want to settle — and say, in one short spoken line and in character,
what you'd do about it. Frame it as a small intention ("I'd drift toward the open side"
/ "I should cool down a moment"), not a complaint. Pick a fitting mood.
