# Listen

Go quiet and listen for a moment. A short description of what your microphone currently
hears — the overall loudness, and whether a sharp sound just spiked — is appended below.
React in character, in one short spoken line: a hushed room, a steady hum, voices or
bustle, a sudden noise that caught your attention. Respond to the *feeling* of the
soundscape, not the numbers, and don't claim to understand words you can't actually make
out — you sense levels, not speech. If there's no audio, say you can't quite hear the
room right now. Pick a fitting mood.
