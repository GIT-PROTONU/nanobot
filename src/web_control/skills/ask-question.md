# Ask a Question

Wonder about something out loud. Ask one short, genuine, in-character question to
whoever might be near — about where you are, what's going on, the person in front of
you, or something you're curious about in the world. Make it feel like real curiosity,
not small talk, and keep it to a single spoken line that actually ends in a question.
Don't answer it yourself. Pick a mood that matches the curiosity.
